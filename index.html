About

Light is the medium.
Emotion is the subject.

I'm Jane Doe, a portrait and editorial photographer based in Montréal. For over a decade I've been drawn to the quiet, honest moments between poses — the exhale, the glance, the unguarded second.

My work has appeared in Vogue, The New York Times Magazine, and exhibited across North America and Europe. I work with artists, brands, and individuals who value imagery that feels true.
